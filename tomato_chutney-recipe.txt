Tomato chutney recipe:

2 ripe tomato
1 teaspoon jeera
1 teaspoon corrainder seeds
2 teaspoon oil
2 thai green chilli
some corrainder leaves
1/2 teaspoon salt

Heat oil in a pan.
Add jeera and corrainder seeds to oil.
Once oil is slightly hot add green chilli.
Then add salt and cut tomato. Saute for a minute.
Put the mixture into a mixer and add corrainder leaves.
Make into a thick paste. And you are ready to serve.
